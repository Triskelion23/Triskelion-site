From Silicon to Steam: The Triskelion Origin

Triskelion Energy was born not in a boardroom, but in a flash of insight — sparked by the same spirit that drove the original NC State Wolfspeed founders. They saw a path to cleaner, more resilient systems through wide bandgap semiconductors. We saw our path in geothermal fire and volcanic steam.

Founded by a scientist-inventor with roots in Norman and Sicilian heritage, Triskelion emerged from a vision to harmonize nature's chaos — not control it. Our goal: to turn Earth's volcanic breath into balanced energy, tapping steam from Mount Etna and similar sites around the world.

Like Wolfspeed, we believe in materials that endure, ideas that scale, and energy that flows. But instead of silicon wafers, we shape pressure, flow, and earth-forged heat into power. Our systems are inspired by nature’s own design — decentralized, adaptive, and always seeking balance.

We are not rivals of Wolfspeed — we are its geothermal cousins. Where they went inward into chips, we reach outward into the crust. Together, we represent two branches of a new world order: one where technology obeys the laws of nature, not just the market.
